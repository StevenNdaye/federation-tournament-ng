African Nations League — Simulator

Build teams, seed a knockout bracket (Quarter-Finals → Semi-Finals → Final), then Play (with commentary) or Simulate matches. Winners auto-advance and federations can be notified by email when results are in.

How to run a demo

  1. Sign in (bottom-right of the top bar if logged out).
  2. As a representative, go to Register and create a federation: choose a country, enter the manager, generate a 23-player squad, pick a captain, submit.
  3. Repeat until you have at least 8 federations.
  4. As an administrator, open Bracket and click Start Tournament. This seeds 4 Quarter-Finals (QF1..QF4).
  5. Open any QF match:
    • Simulate: instant result + goal scorers.
    • Play: generates a 90’ timeline; ET/Pens if needed.
  6. On completion, the winner auto-advances: QF1+QF2 → SF1, QF3+QF4 → SF2, then SF1+SF2 → Final.
  7. (Optional) If federation emails were provided, the platform sends an email with the result.
  8. View global rankings in Top Scorers.
  9. Need a fresh run? From Bracket, click New Tournament (admin only) to wipe and re-seed from Quarter-Finals.

Player ratings & team strength

Each player has a natural position (GK, DF, MD, AT). Ratings are auto-assigned: natural position → 50–100, other positions → 0–50. A team’s overall rating is the average across its 23 players.

Tips for a smooth demo

  • Have 7 federations pre-registered; add the 8th live during the demo.
  • Show both flows: one Simulate and one Play with commentary.
  • Point out winners are highlighted (green) and losers (red) on the bracket after results.
  • Show the email notification (if you captured representative emails).
  • Finish by resetting with New Tournament to prove repeatability.
